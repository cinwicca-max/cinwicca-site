Cinwicca - site files (Netlify ready)
=====================================

TO PUBLISH:
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag this whole folder (or its zip) onto the page
3. Netlify gives you a link like cinwicca.netlify.app

THE FORMS:
This site uses Netlify Forms (no Formspree needed).
After your first deploy, the form requests arrive in:
  Netlify dashboard -> your site -> Forms tab
Two forms are set up: "soul-path" (booking) and "reflection" (testimonials).
To also get them by e-mail: Netlify -> Forms -> Form notifications ->
  add e-mail notification to cinwicca@gmail.com

FILES:
- index.html    (main page; was "Soul Blueprint.html")
- i18n.js       (EN / PT-BR / ES translations)
- samples/      (the two veiled sample readings)
