The Work

A practice of integrity

This work is deeply personal, and part of my own experiment as a Seer — a Human Design Projector. With my focused aura, I am here to truly see you, and to guide you in the areas you need most, desire most, and long for most.

The process may validate parts of yourself you always knew were there, but repressed because no one else recognised them. It may also shine light on patterns you developed under pressure or conditioning — parts that don't truly belong to you.

Together, we will uncover the real blueprint of your Soul: who you are, the gifts you carry from ancestry and past lives, and the path your Soul is moving toward.
